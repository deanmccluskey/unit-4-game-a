Crystal Collector!

At the start of the game, you will be given a random target number for you to match.

There are four crystals below with four hidden values. These values change at the beginning of each round. By clicking on a crystal, you add its value to your total score.

You win the round by matching your total score to the target number. You lose the round if your total score goes over the target number.

Player wins and losses are then tallyed and displayed.
